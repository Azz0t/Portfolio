Fuss

Fuss is a web application I developed during a university project to create a platform similar to Jira or Trello for project management.


Users can :

Manage multiple projects with a Kanban board to create, organize, and move tasks.

Track progress by tasks, epics, and individual team members.

Visualize the project timeline with a roadmap.
